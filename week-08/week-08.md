Week 8

This week we touched on data regulations and GDPR. For our project, the only user information we are working with is health data from their watch. In our system, this data is stored locally, which creates no issues with GDPR as the data is not sold or sent anywhere.

We talked about accessibility standards and how we can make our project as accessible as possible to everyone. We decided on adding more settings for users to customise their experience. These include reducing motion on the screen and changing font size.
