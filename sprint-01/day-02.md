Tuesday 17th February

Myself, Jake and Asher continued to work on and finished our figma prototype, deciding how we wanted the app to look

Once we were happy with our plans, we began development.

I focused on making a style file, which could be imported into other files to standardise the design across our app. I specified the font and text colour of page titles, box titles and general text. I then chose a background colour and a complementary colour for widgets and other objects, and some standard styling for the homepage buttons such as rounded corners and shadows. I also styled the navigation bar to match the rest of the app.
